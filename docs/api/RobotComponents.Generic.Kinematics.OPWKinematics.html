Remarks

Based on the paper 'An Analytical Solution of the Inverse Kinematics Problem of Industrial Serial Manipulators with an Ortho-parallel Basis and a Spherical Wrist' by Mathias Brandstötter, Arthur Angerer, and Michael Hofbaur.

Solution order:

Sol. Wrist center Wrist center Axis 5 angle relative to axis 1 relative to lower arm

0 In front of In front of Positive 1 In front of Behind Positive 2 Behind In front of Positive 3 Behind Behind Positive 4 In front of In front of Negative 5 In front of Behind Negative 6 Behind In front of Negative 7 Behind Behind Negative
